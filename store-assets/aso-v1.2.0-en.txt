★ Description (4000 characters max)
================================================================

📊 Turn your trading history into real improvement

Whether you're new to forex or you've been trading for years, keeping a
trading journal is one of the fastest ways to raise your win rate.
FX Trade Journal is a serious trade-tracking app built for traders who
want to actually learn from their history — including direct MT4/MT5
trade history import.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ Log
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

・Log a trade in one tap: currency pair, entry price, lot size, P/L, and pips
・Attach chart screenshots to any trade
・Add notes and tags for easy review later
・Bulk-import your MT4 / MT5 trade history CSV (new)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ Analyze
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

・Monthly dashboard auto-calculates win rate, pips, profit factor, and P/L
・Weekly, per-pair, and time-of-day performance breakdowns
・Daily streak tracking to keep your recording habit alive

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ Share
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

・Turn your monthly results into a clean, shareable stats card (X, Instagram, and more)
・PRO removes the watermark for a fully clean design

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ Who it's for
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

・Traders just starting out who want to build a recording habit
・MT4/MT5 users tired of manual spreadsheet tracking
・Anyone who wants a monthly view of their win rate and pips
・Traders who like sharing their results on social media
・Anyone who wants a trading journal that lives entirely on their phone

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ FX Trade Journal PRO
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

A monthly or annual subscription unlocks the premium tier:

・Weekly and deep-dive insight analytics
・Watermark-free share cards
・Premium-only detailed statistics
・All future premium features

Pricing is shown on the purchase confirmation screen.
Subscriptions can be cancelled anytime from your device's Settings app.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ About your data
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

・All data is stored locally on your device
・No cloud sync required, no internet connection needed to use the app
・Built with a privacy-first design

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
■ Supported brokers / platforms
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Any broker that lets you export MT4/MT5-format trade history CSV works
with the app. Manual entry is fully supported too, for any broker or
platform.

 Notes for whoever publishes this
================================================================

1. This locale does not exist yet in App Store Connect. Adding it
   requires creating a NEW app version (the current v1.2.0 is already
   approved/live and its metadata is locked — see project memory
   "App Store Connectの仕様上の制約"). Bundle this with the next
   feature-update release.
2. Screenshots must be re-captured with the app's language set to
   English (src/i18n/en.ts is already implemented, so this is just a
   matter of switching the device/simulator language before shooting).
3. Legal pages are ready at:
   - privacy-policy-en.html
   - terms-en.html
   - support-en.html
   These need to be deployed to the same host as the Japanese versions
   (GitHub Pages) and linked from the English App Store Connect listing
   (Support URL / Privacy Policy URL fields) before submission.
4. IMPORTANT: privacy-policy.html (Japanese) currently states the app is
   "provided targeting users in Japan" (本アプリは日本国内のユーザーを対象として
   提供しています). This sentence was intentionally omitted from the
   English version since it directly contradicts a global release —
   confirm this is the intended direction before publishing.
5. Pricing: Apple auto-converts JPY tiers to other currencies. Recommend
   spot-checking that the auto-converted USD/EUR/GBP price points land on
   sensible psychological price points (e.g. $4.99/$29.99-style endings)
   before submission — see output/ideas/marketing_strategy_20260714.md
   section on international pricing.
