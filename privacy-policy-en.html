Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

"FX Trade Journal" (the "App") helps individual traders record and review their FX trades. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect and Use

The App collects and uses the following information:

  • Trade record data: currency pairs, profit/loss, rates, notes, and other trade information you enter (stored on your device)
  • Chart images: photos you optionally attach (stored on your device)
  • App settings: notification times, lot size units, account balance, and other settings (stored on your device)
  • Purchase and billing information: subscription purchase status and plan information (processed via RevenueCat)

2. Information We Do Not Use

  • We do not use location data
  • We do not access the microphone or camera
  • No behavioral-advertising SDK is embedded in the App

3. Data Shared with Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, and some data is transmitted to their servers.

RevenueCat RevenueCat, Inc. (USA) Anonymous user ID, purchase history, plan status, device identifier Processing, managing, and restoring subscription purchases

International data transfers: As a result of the transmissions above, your data may be processed outside your country of residence, including in the United States. These providers are subject to their own standard data protection terms (such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses or the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, where applicable).

4. Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device's photo library so you can attach chart images to your trade records. Selected images are copied into the App's own storage. The App does not scan your entire library or access any images other than the ones you select.

5. Trade History File Access (MT4/MT5 CSV Import)

The App requests access to files you choose in order to import MT4 or MT5 trade history CSV files. Imported data is stored only on your device and is never sent to an external server.

6. Notifications

The App uses notification permissions to send "recording reminders" at the time you set. If you enable weekly summary notifications, a performance recap notification is sent every Monday. Notification content is generated entirely on your device.

7. Data Management

All trade records and settings are stored in a local SQLite database on your device. Uninstalling the App deletes all of this data. A CSV export feature is available for backup purposes; you choose the destination yourself.

8. Social Sharing Feature

The App lets you share monthly performance images to social media. Sharing only happens when you explicitly choose to do so, and you can review the shared content before sending it. Images shared on the free plan may include the App's logo (watermark). The paid plan (PRO) allows you to share clean images without a watermark.

9. Affiliate Links

The App's settings screen may include affiliate links introducing third-party FX broker account opening services. If you use a service through such a link, the operator may receive a referral fee. Any personal information you provide to a linked service is provided by you directly to that service, not through the App. Click and referral data collected through these links is handled according to each affiliate service's own privacy policy.

10. Information Collected During Beta Testing

When you apply to participate in closed testing, you may be asked to provide a Gmail address for tester invitations. This email address is used solely to send the tester invitation and for no other purpose.

11. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA and Other Regions)

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing, and request a copy of your data in a portable format. Because almost all app data (trade records, settings, etc.) is stored only on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly by using the in-app export/delete features or by uninstalling the App. For the limited data we hold ourselves (such as a beta tester's email address), contact us using the details in Section 17 and we will respond within 2 business days. If you are in the EU/EEA/UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

If you are a California resident, please note: we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, and we do not use any behavioral-advertising SDK in the App.

12. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA, where applicable), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

13. Requests Regarding Personal Data

For requests to disclose, correct, add to, delete, or stop using/sharing any personal data we hold (such as an email address collected during beta testing), please contact us at the email address below. After verifying your identity, we will generally respond within 2 business days.

Trade records and other in-app data are stored only on your own device and are not accessible to the operator. Such data can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

14. Security Measures

The operator takes reasonable and appropriate security measures — such as preventing unauthorized access and managing passwords securely — to prevent the leakage, loss, or damage of any personal information collected (such as beta tester email addresses).

15. Disclaimer

The App does not provide investment advice. We do not guarantee the accuracy of any analytics displayed (win rate, pips, profit factor, etc.), and we accept no responsibility for investment decisions or losses. Please trade at your own discretion and risk.
