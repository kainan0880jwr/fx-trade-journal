Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Where is my recorded data stored?
A. All data is stored locally on your device. It is never automatically sent to an external server. When switching devices, use the JSON backup feature in Settings to export your data, then import it on your new device.

Q. How do I cancel my subscription?
A. iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FX Trade Journal.
Android: Google Play app → Menu → Subscriptions → FX Trade Journal.

Q. My purchase isn't showing up.
A. Try the "Restore Purchases" button in the App. If the issue persists, please contact us with details about your device and OS version.

Q. Can I import MT4/MT5 trade history?
A. Yes — go to Settings to import an MT4-format CSV file. Formats vary between brokers, so some files may not import correctly.
